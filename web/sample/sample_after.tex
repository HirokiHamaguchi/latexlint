For a family of sets $\{X_i\}_1^{23}$, consider $Y \coloneqq \bigcup_{i=1}^{23} X_i$.
Let $F\colon \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{R}$ be a mapping defined by
\begin{equation}
    F(n) \coloneqq \sqrt{ \max( n, 10 ) },
\end{equation}
and define $z=F(\# Y)$, which is not meaningful.
