For a family of sets $\{X_i\}_1^23$, consider $Y := \cup_{i=1}^23 X_i$.
Let $F: \mathbb{Z} \to \mathbb{R}$ be a mapping defined by
\begin{align}
    F(n) := & \sqrt( \max{ n, 10 } ),\\
\end{align}
and define $z=F(\sharp Y)$, which is not meaningful.
