\section{LLSpaceEnglish}

Missing spaces around inline math should be detected.

Improper spacing: a\(x\)b and a$x$b.
Proper spacing: a \(x\) b and a $x$ b.

The following are excluded by rule exceptions: $n$th, \(m\)th, $\backslash$n, \(\backslash\)m, H\({}_2\)O, C\({}_{n}\)H\({}_{2n+2}\).\\
No detection in the following cases:  1$x$, 1\(x\), $x$1, \(x\)1, $x$, \(x\), etc.
