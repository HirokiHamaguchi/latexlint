For a family of sets $\{X_i\}_1^{23}$, consider $Y \coloneqq \bigcup_{i=1}^{23} X_i$.

Let $F\colon Y \to \mathbb{Z}$ be a mapping defined by
\begin{align}
    F(Y) \coloneqq{}& \sqrt{ \max( \# Y, 10 ) },\\
    G(Y) \coloneqq{}& F(Y)^2.
\end{align}
